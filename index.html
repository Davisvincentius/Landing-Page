It's not just coffee
It's Starbucks

Starbucks sells hot and cold drinks, coffee beans, salads, hot and cold sandwiches, sweet pastries, snacks, and items like glasses and tumblers. Through its Starbucks Entertainment division and the Hear Music brand, the company also markets books, music, and films. Many of the company's products are seasonal or specific to the area where the shop is located/ Starbucks ice cream and coffee are also sold at grocery stores.
